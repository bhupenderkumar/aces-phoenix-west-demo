Our Mission & History

Aces-Phoenix West was established in 2010 to meet the growing demand for specialized education in Phoenix. Our school has grown to serve 187 students with diverse needs, providing a supportive community for families and educators alike.

Our teaching approach is centered around social-emotional learning, with a focus on building strong relationships and fostering a growth mindset in our students. We use evidence-based practices to tailor instruction to meet the unique needs of each child.

In addition to rigorous academics, we offer a range of extracurricular activities and character development programs designed to promote social, emotional, and physical growth. Our students participate in sports, music, art, and community service projects to develop important life skills.
