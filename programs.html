Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is designed for students aged 3-4, with a focus on play-based learning and social-emotional development. Our experienced teachers provide a nurturing environment that fosters curiosity and creativity.

📚

Elementary

Our elementary program serves students in grades K-4, with a focus on building strong foundational skills in reading, writing, math, and science. Our teachers use engaging, interactive approaches to make learning fun and accessible.

🔍

Middle/Upper

Our middle and upper school program serves students in grades 5-8, with a focus on building on foundational skills and introducing more complex concepts in language arts, math, science, and social studies. Our teachers use project-based learning approaches to promote critical thinking and problem-solving.

🧠

STEM

Our STEM program is designed for students who show a passion for science, technology, engineering, and math. Our teachers use hands-on, project-based approaches to develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

🎨

Arts

Our arts program provides students with opportunities to explore their creative side through music, art, drama, and dance. Our teachers use engaging, interactive approaches to foster imagination and self-expression.

🏈

Athletics

Our athletics program provides students with opportunities to develop physical skills and teamwork through sports such as basketball, soccer, and track and field. Our coaches use positive, supportive approaches to promote sportsmanship and personal growth.
